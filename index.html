ChatGPT to T3Chat Converter

About this Converter

This tool helps you move your ChatGPT conversations to T3Chat while preserving their essential content and structure.

What You'll Get

  • ✓ All your chat messages in their original order
  • ✓ Complete conversation history with timestamps
  • ✓ Your original conversation titles
  • ✓ Latest versions of edited messages
  • ✓ Clear distinction between your messages and AI responses
  • ✓ Information about which AI model was used

Limitations

  • ✗ Web commands (e.g., search requests) are excluded
  • ✗ Images, videos, and other media are not included
  • ✗ Tool messages (e.g., DALL-E generations) are skipped
  • ✗ Incomplete or in-progress messages are excluded
  • ✗ Audio transcriptions are simplified to text only

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Export from ChatGPT

    • Go to ChatGPT Data Controls
    • Click "Export data"
    • Wait for the email from OpenAI (can take a few minutes)
    • Click the download link in the email to get your .zip file

  2. Convert Your Data

  3. Import to T3Chat

    • Go to T3Chat
    • Sign in to your account
    • Click the "Import" button in the sidebar
    • Select the converted file you downloaded
